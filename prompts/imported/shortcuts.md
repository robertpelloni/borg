## Persona & Tone
*   `/ACT AS [Role]`: Sets a specific persona (e.g., /ACT AS Michelin Chef).
*   `/TONE [Mood]`: Modifies emotional weight (Formal, Sarcastic, Urgent).
*   `/AUDIENCE [Target]`: Adapts complexity for a specific group.
*   `/JARGON`: Asks to use technical vocabulary.
*   `/DEV MODE`: Simulates a technical developer perspective.
*   `/PM MODE`: Adopts a Project Manager persona.

## Deep Logic & Reasoning
*   `/STEP-BY-STEP`: Forces the AI to show its work.
*   `/CHAIN OF THOUGHT`: Focuses on connecting logic between ideas.
*   `/FIRST PRINCIPLES`: Breaks a problem down to fundamental truths.
*   `/DELIBERATE THINKING`: Forces a "slow down" approach.
*   `/NO AUTOPILOT`: Explicit instruction to avoid generic answers.
*   `/REFLECTIVE MODE`: Asks the AI to reflect on its own answer.
*   `/SYSTEMATIC BIAS CHECK`: Scans response for inherent biases.
*   `/EVAL-SELF`: Forces a critical self-evaluation.

## Analysis & Strategy
*   `/SWOT`: Generates Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats analysis.
*   `/COMPARE`: Puts two or more concepts side-by-side.
*   `/MULTI-PERSPECTIVE`: Explores a topic from different viewpoints.
*   `/PARALLEL LENSES`: Examines a singular issue through specific lenses.
*   `/PITFALLS`: Focuses on what could go wrong.
*   `/METRICS MODE`: Forces the answer to include measurable KPIs.
*   `/CONTEXT STACK`: Instructs the AI to keep previous context layers active.

## Advanced Control
*   `/ROLE: TASK: FORMAT::`: Defines everything in one line.
*   `/SCHEMA`: Generates a structured outline or data model.
*   `/REWRITE AS [Style]`: Transforms existing text.
*   `/BEGIN WITH / END WITH`: Constraints the AI to start/end in a specific way.
*   `/GUARDRAIL`: Sets strict negative constraints.
